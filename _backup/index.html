Free whiteboard for sketches and diagrams

Draw by NousVault is a free browser whiteboard for sketching ideas, mapping flows, making simple diagrams, and capturing visual notes. Pen, shapes, arrows, text, undo, redo, import, and export — all frontend-only with no account and no tracking.

This online whiteboard is built for quick thinking and lightweight collaboration prep. Use it for product planning, study notes, UI wireframes, technical diagrams, or rough brainstorming sessions directly from desktop or mobile browser.

Because the app is frontend-only, it is useful for fast visual thinking without signup friction. You can open the whiteboard, sketch a flow, place labels, export a PNG, or keep a rough diagram locally when you need speed more than a heavy collaborative workspace.

Typical use cases include architecture sketches, journey maps, system diagrams, classroom notes, feature planning, and quick wireframes. The interface stays minimal on purpose — a clean canvas for focused thinking.
